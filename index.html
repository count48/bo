About Us

who are we?

We ‘BlackOrange Prefabs’ are one of the market leaders engaged in manufacturing, supplying and exporting a wide assortment of Pre engineered and pre fabricated structures. These products are designed and developed in our latest infrastructure facility.

Our highly skilful and experienced workers make use of the best grade quality raw materials such as mild steel and stainless steel, procured from reliable vendors across the country. The entire range of products are extensively used in diverse areas such as warehouses, factories, industries, workshops, Defence, hospitals, brigdes, educational institutions,airlines and cargo stations.
